Gitolite webui

Welcom to your one stop show for managing all of you access to git repositories, in order to gain access you need to upload your public key and then request access to specific repositories, once your requests are approved you will be notified via an email.

Upload public key

Public keys can be easily generated on Nix systems using: 

$ ssh-keygen

Once the key is generated its located within: 

$ ls ~/.ssh 
   config   id_rsa  id_rsa.pub  known_hosts
id_rsa.pub is the one you need to upload, on Windows machine you may follow this guide.
